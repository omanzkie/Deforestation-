Deforestation

Deforestation is the large-scale removal of forests, usually to make way for agriculture, urban development, or industrial use.

Causes

Deforestation is mainly caused by agriculture, logging, urbanization, mining, fires, cattle ranching, overpopulation, and infrastructure development.These activities lead to large-scale forest loss, harming ecosystems and biodiversity.

Effects

Deforestation causes climate change, loss of animals and plants, soil erosion, less rainfall, floods, poor air quality, job loss, and desertification.

Solutions

To stop deforestation, we can plant trees, use sustainable logging, protect forests, farm responsibly, reduce wood use, support eco-friendly products, enforce laws, and educate people.
